How Our AI Engine Works


Our AI-driven plant disease detection system leverages advanced deep learning models to analyze plant images and detect potential diseases with high accuracy. The process follows these key steps: Image Capture & Upload Users capture or upload an image of their plant leaves showing signs of disease. The system supports multiple image formats and optimizes the image for analysis. AI-Powered Image Processing The uploaded image is processed using computer vision techniques. The model detects leaf patterns, color variations, and structural abnormalities. Deep Learning Disease Classification The AI model, trained on thousands of plant disease samples, classifies the detected symptoms. It predicts whether the plant is healthy or suffering from diseases like fungal infections, bacterial spots, or nutrient deficiencies. Real-Time Diagnosis & Recommendations The system provides an instant diagnosis with a confidence score. Users receive personalized recommendations, including treatment options and preventive measures. Continuous Learning & Improvement The AI continuously improves by learning from new user-uploaded images. Regular updates enhance detection accuracy and expand the model's knowledge base.
